About

This web application takes the top videos from Reddit and makes a playlist that you can watch without having to touch the keyboard or mouse.

Reddit is an online community that rates and discusses content. Stuff that makes it to the top of Reddit is generally worth watching, so it is a pretty effective way to screen online videos for interesting content. Occasionally, stuff that may be objectionable to some viewers, e.g. NSFW (not safe for work) or NSFL (not safe for life -- gore) videos do make it to the top of the list, so viewer discretion is still recommended.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Why is the playlist out of date with what's on /r/videos? In order to reduce the burden on Reddit, this application keeps a local cache of the top 100 entries. This cache is updated every 30 minutes or so.
  2. Why are some videos being skipped? Besides YouTube, Reddit links to videos on various other sites such as Vimeo, Dailymotion, and others. Support for viewing videos on these sites is currently not implemented, so such videos are skipped.
